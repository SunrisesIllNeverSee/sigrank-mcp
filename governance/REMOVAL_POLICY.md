# Removal policy

Operators should have a self-serve path to pause collection, delete history, or delete an account. The interface must explain scope, consequences, and expected completion state before confirmation.

If self-service is unavailable or inaccessible, contact the SignalAF support/privacy channel published in the application. Requests should be authenticated where feasible, tracked to completion, and answered with the action taken or a clear reason for any lawful exception.

Removal includes downstream public views and future release queues where technically possible. Backups and security logs may expire on their retention schedule rather than being rewritten immediately; they must not be restored into active service except as permitted by the retention policy.

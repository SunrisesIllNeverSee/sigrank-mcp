# Data policy

## Collected

SignalAF may receive submission-window metadata, four raw token pillars, session/activity aggregates, device and agent integrity material, signed snapshot data, and account-to-operator links needed to provide the service.

## Derived

The service derives cascade metrics, scoring outputs, ranks, percentiles, verification tiers, integrity reasons, and field aggregates. Derived data remains conditional on its source window and method.

## Public

The live board may present operator-facing profile and metric information subject to product visibility and opt-in rules. Research, datasets, reports, and downloads must use anonymized `signal-##########` identifiers and must not include direct identity fields.

Collection is not permission to repurpose data. New uses require a documented basis, minimization review, and any required renewed consent.

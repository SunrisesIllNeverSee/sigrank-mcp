# Ethics

- **User agency:** people control participation, visibility where available, and deletion choices.
- **No silent collection:** telemetry collection requires a clear, affirmative product interaction and documented purpose.
- **Transparency:** explain measurements, verification scope, uncertainty, and policy changes in direct language.
- **Non-maleficence:** do not frame telemetry as a measure of human worth, employability, intelligence, or intent.
- **Data containment:** research-facing materials use anonymized identifiers and exclude direct identity data.
- **Integrity without accusation:** flags indicate review or reduced confidence, not misconduct.

The observatory should make useful patterns inspectable without treating people as raw material for a leaderboard.

# Roadmap

## Near term

- Make the observatory spine the shared, versioned vocabulary for the app and MCP.
- Strengthen consent, deletion, provenance, and verification surfaces.
- Release methodology notes alongside every public metric surface.

## Atlas

The Atlas will describe behavioral profiles as evidence-backed patterns, not fixed identities. Profiles will be optional interpretations over defined windows and methods.

## Dataset releases

Future releases will be versioned, documented, and privacy-reviewed. Research-facing records must use anonymized identifiers and exclude direct identity fields unless a separately documented, explicit publication basis exists.

## Research

Research will distinguish observation, computation, and interpretation; publish limitations; and support replication from a documented release. `research/` and `papers/` are placeholders until an active release or publication exists.

# Principles

1. **Immutable observations.** Preserve submitted observations and their context; correct derived views through explicit recomputation rather than rewriting history.
2. **Reproducible metrics.** Public cascade diagnostics are pure functions of four raw pillars. Their definitions and guards are inspectable.
3. **Transparent methodology.** Publish formulas, thresholds that are public, validation scope, and limitations. Do not present a heuristic as a fact.
4. **User agency.** Collection is explicit; operators can control submission, visibility, claims, and removal paths.
5. **Scientific humility.** SignalAF measures bounded telemetry patterns, not talent, intent, or human worth. Findings remain contingent on coverage, verification, and method version.

Private anti-gaming parameters and server-only rules are not disclosed when disclosure would enable manipulation; that boundary must itself be documented.
